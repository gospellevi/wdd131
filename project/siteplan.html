Site Name

The site name is LEGOS Consult.

This name represents an educational consulting agency that is focused on international schools admission and visa processing, international school payment services, and English Testing preparation and payment services.

Site Purpose

To provide comprehensive information about the services offered, including:

  • International schools admission applications
  • Visa processing and fee payments
  • International school payment services (admission fees, SEVIS, school fees, WES fees, etc.)
  • English Testing preparation classes (TOEFL, IELTS, CELPIP, PTE, DUOLINGO Test, etc.) and exam fee payments

Scenarios

  1. How can I apply for admission to an international school through LEGOS Consult?
  2. What are the steps involved in visa processing, and how can LEGOS Consult assist me?
  3. What English tests do you offer preparation classes for, and how can I register for these classes?
