Our Expert Services

International School Admissions

Assistance with applications to schools abroad and guidance on choosing the right school and program. International School Payments

Handling various payments like admission fees, SEVIS, school fees, WES fees, etc. We streamline the payment process to ensure timely and accurate transactions. Our services provide peace of mind, allowing you to focus on your studies without the hassle of financial logistics.

Study Visa Application Processing

Step-by-step guidance on visa application processes and assistance with visa fee payments. Our experienced team ensures that all necessary documentation is accurately prepared and submitted. We provide ongoing support throughout the process, helping you navigate any challenges and secure your visa successfully.

Test of English Preparation

Information on TOEFL, IELTS, CELPIP, PTE, DUOLINGO Test, etc., and enrollment details for preparation classes and exam fee payments. Our comprehensive preparation programs are designed to help you achieve your best possible scores. We offer expert guidance, practice materials, and personalized coaching to ensure your success in these crucial language proficiency tests.
